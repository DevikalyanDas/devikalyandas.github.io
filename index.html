Devikalyan Das

I am a Masters student at Universität des Saarlandes in the department of Visual Computing. I am also working as a student Research Assisstant towards building deeplearning techniques for efficient grading of Cancer Histopathology images at ANNA/C-TRUS GmbH and DFKI, Kaiserlautern under the supervision of Dr. Gerd Reis. My research interest lies in the intersection of Computer Graphics, Computer Vision and Machine Learning towards designing algorithms for efficient understanding and rendering of the 3D scene around me. Before coming here, I was a Research Fellow at National Institute of Technology, Surathkal, India, where I worked with Dr. Shyam Lal on designing Automated Cancer Detection System using efficient Deep Learning algorithms.

Previously, I was a software engineer at Tech Mahindra mainly involved in data analysis and visualization using Python. I completed my Bachelors in Electronics & Telecommunications from VSSUT Burla.

Lastly, I am always an advocate of open-source research. Apart from academia, I like reading books, playing flute, and travelling.
